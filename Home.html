Advantages Of
Liquid Fertilizers

  • Immediate availability of nutrients to plants.
  • No risk of nitrogen loss during surface application.
  • Can be applied in mixture with pesticides/bio stimulants and agrochemicals.
  • Increased utilisation of micro nutrients from the soil.
